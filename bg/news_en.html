What's new!

New series of IC engine trucks... 
as of May 2007, trucks with KUBOTA and DEUTZ engines are in production. They meet all the requirements of Stage III and Stage II for the emissions of exhaust gases

Testing... 
is under way of the new development of Balkancar-Record’s electric trucks with a lifting capacity of 1.4-1.6 t and a lifting height of up to 6.4 m. With its high manoeuvrability and compact dimensions, it is suitable for storehouses. It will be in full-scale production as from the second half of 2007

A new combined device... 
for IC engine trucks and tow tractors, allowing control and monitoring of their operation. The device informs the truck operator about the need for servicing. In the event of any malfunction, the device gives the truck operator a sound and light alarm, while at the same time recording the event in its memory
